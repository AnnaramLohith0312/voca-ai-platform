VOCA must feel like a premium career intelligence platform, not a quiz, not a job board, and not a generic AI startup template. ## Product Intent VOCA converts scattered self-knowledge into structured career direction through a guided, explainable, and adaptive experience. The product should feel less like completing a form and more like having a calm, intelligent conversation with a high-agency advisor. The user should feel understood, guided, respected, and more certain about what to explore next by the end of the flow. ## Product Layers VOCA has four primary product layers: - Trust layer 芒聙聰 landing, authentication, privacy reassurance, and explainability. - Conversation layer 芒聙聰 guided intake through chat-style prompts. - Adaptive intelligence layer 芒聙聰 branching question flow and dynamic skill surfacing. - Recommendation layer 芒聙聰 explainable matches, skill gaps, role details, and next-step guidance. ## Core Principles - Guided, not form-like 芒聙聰 onboarding must use one-question-at-a-time interactions rather than a static survey or multi-field form. - Serious, not futuristic-noisy 芒聙聰 the interface should use dark cinematic surfaces, restrained glow, teal action color, muted gold emphasis, and minimal visual clutter. - Adaptive, not generic 芒聙聰 the interface should clearly communicate that VOCA is tailoring the experience based on previous answers and emerging confidence signals. - Mobile-first intelligence 芒聙聰 the entire experience must work beautifully on phones, especially chat, answer cards, skill rating, and results exploration. - Explainability everywhere 芒聙聰 whenever VOCA asks something or recommends something, the interface should help the user understand why it matters. ## Brand Tone VOCA芒聙聶s tone is: **cinematic clarity meets quiet precision**. The product should feel cinematic, intelligent, warm-dark, premium, calm, precise, and human. Every design choice should reinforce trust, focus, clarity, and purposeful structure rather than decoration. ## Information Architecture ### Public Pages - `/` 芒聙聰 landing page - `/auth` 芒聙聰 sign in and sign up ### Guided Experience - `/onboarding/chat` 芒聙聰 separate conversational intake page - `/analysis` 芒聙聰 animated processing state - `/results/:sessionId` 芒聙聰 personalized recommendation output - `/career/:careerSlug` 芒聙聰 detailed role breakdown ### Logged-in Workspace - `/dashboard` 芒聙聰 saved results and recent sessions - `/profile` 芒聙聰 editable user profile - `/settings` 芒聙聰 privacy, account, and preferences ## Experience Flow Landing 芒聠聮 Auth 芒聠聮 Chat onboarding 芒聠聮 Adaptive skills 芒聠聮 Career priorities 芒聠聮 Analysis 芒聠聮 Results 芒聠聮 Save / Compare / Dashboard ## Updated Onboarding Sequence The old multi-step onboarding form is replaced with a conversational, adaptive sequence. - VOCA greets the user. - The user answers one lightweight question. - VOCA chooses the next best question. - Once enough signal is collected, VOCA opens a deeper skill calibration layer. - The user completes career priorities and preference calibration. - VOCA transitions into analysis. - The results page returns ranked, explainable career matches. --- # Visual System ## Color System VOCA uses a dark-first palette with teal as the primary signal color and muted gold as the score and highlight color. ```css :root { --voca-bg: #0b0e12; Landing Page `/` ### Goal Communicate VOCA as a premium career intelligence platform rather than a simple quiz. ### Structure - Sticky navbar. - Full-height hero section. - Problem framing with three pain points. - How VOCA works section. - Intelligence dimensions section. - Sample results preview. - Trust, privacy, and explainability section. - Final CTA strip. - Footer. ### Hero Requirements - Dark cinematic background with subtle trajectory or network motion. - Strong, high-conviction headline. - Short subline explaining guided, personalized career clarity. - One dominant CTA, such as 芒聙聹Start your career map芒聙聺 or 芒聙聹Get my career map.芒聙聺 - One secondary CTA for sample results preview. ### Navbar Requirements - Transparent on hero, then surface + blur on scroll. - Left side should hold the VOCA logo and wordmark. - Right side should hold Sign in and Get started actions. - Mobile should use a hamburger with a slide-down menu. ## 2.
